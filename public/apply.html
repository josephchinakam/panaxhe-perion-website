Join the next cohort of leaders. Please read the instructions below carefully before applying.

Application Process

1. Choose your Discipline

Review our discipline catalog and identify that it aligns with your career goals.

For information you can contact us on our whatsApp contact given on this page.

2. Prepare Documents

You will need the following documents in PDF format:

  • National ID or Passport Copy
  • Academic Transcripts / Certificates
  • Updated CV or Resume
  • A 500-word Motivation Letter

NOTE! Please verify that you meet the specific discipline requirements before submitting any documents. Once verified, contact us via the provided WhatsApp number to confirm the correct file submission formats and letter-writing guidelines.

3. Submit Application & Payment

Do not pay the submission fee when submitting your application. You will be contacted to make this payment only after qualifying for an interview. Payment must be cleared at least 72 hours before the scheduled interview.

Submit via Email

Currently contact our whatsApp number for email details, and make sure you strictly adhere to the format specified by instruction you will be given.

Feel free to call us during business hours on the same line (Except on Sunday Only)
